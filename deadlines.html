Important Dates

  • Wednesday, January 1: New Year's Day - University Closed
  • Monday, January 6: Start of Term
  • Monday-Friday, January 6-10: Block Week
  • Monday, January 13: Start of Classes
  • Wednesday, January 15: Deadline to Apply for Winter Conferral of Degree
  • Thursday, January 23: Last day to drop a class without financial penalty, End of refund period
  • Friday, January 24: Last day to add or swap a course
  • Friday, January 31: Tuition and Fee Payment Deadline
  • Thursday, February 13: Winter Conferral of Degree
  • Sunday-Saturday, February 16-22: Term Break - No Classes
  • Monday, February 17: Alberta Family Day
  • Monday, March 31: Deadline to Apply for Spring Convocation
  • Friday, April 11: End of Classes, Last day to withdraw from a course
  • Monday, April 14: Start of Exams
  • Friday, April 18: Good Friday - University Closed
  • Monday, April 21: Easter Monday - University Closed
  • Monday, April 28: End of Exams
  • Wednesday, April 30: End of Term
